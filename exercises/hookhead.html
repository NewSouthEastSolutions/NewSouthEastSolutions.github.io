Welcome to Hook Head Lighthouse

Explore History, Adventure, and Family Fun

Guided Tours

Join one of our expert guides as they lead you through the fascinating history of Hook Head Lighthouse. Learn about the ancient origins of this iconic landmark and its role in maritime navigation.

Our tours are suitable for all ages and provide a unique insight into the lighthouse's past and present. Don't miss the chance to climb to the top for a panoramic view of the surrounding coastline!

Breathtaking Views

Experience the stunning natural beauty of County Waterford from the vantage point of Hook Head Lighthouse. Marvel at the rugged cliffs, rolling waves, and diverse wildlife that call this area home.

Whether you're an avid photographer or simply looking to relax and take in the scenery, Hook Head offers unparalleled views that will leave you in awe.
